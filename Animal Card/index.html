GERMAN SHEPHERD

Age: 6 yrs
Colour: Tan
Weight: 10 - 15 pounds

The German Shepherd is a highly intelligent dog breed known for its strength, agility, and loyalty.These dogs are native to Germany and widely used today in various roles, including police and military work. They have a distinctive appearance with a strong and well-proportioned body, erect ears, and a thick double coat. They are known for their trainability, protective instincts, and ability to form strong bonds with their owners. They are often praised for their courage, work ethic, and adaptability, making them one of the most popular and respected dog breeds worldwide.
